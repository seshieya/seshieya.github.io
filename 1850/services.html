Services

Custom Design

Finishings

TeamArt Millwork can provide an estimate based on existing design drawings or create drawings tailored to your needs. We have inhouse designers that will consult with you every step of the way.

Our inhouse experts can use various techniques to achieve the look you desire. Finishings include staining, painting, custom colour matching, and more.

CNC Millwork

Wall Panels & Ceilings

We operate a CNC machine that can carve any design and create intricate patterns.

Elaborate wall panels and ceiling millwork are among our specialties.

Door Restoration

We can repair or recreate your existing door to match the other doors in your office or home.
