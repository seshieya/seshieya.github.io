FFXV Review

Final Fantasy XV: A Visually Stunning and Action-Packed RPG

Posted by Angela Wu

Final Fantasy XV (FFXV) is the latest installment of the Final Fantasy video game series. It is a Role-Playing Game (RPG) which is a type of game where the player assumes the role of the protagonist, and follows the story of this character. In FFXV, you control the main protagonist Noctis, and you explore the world that he lives in. FFXV was in development for 10 years, and was finally released on Nov 29, 2016.

FFXV features amazing graphics and beautiful scenery that are inspired by our real-world scenery. Here is a video montage showing some of the breath-taking environment footage from the game: FFXV World of Wonder Environment Footage

The below images on this page feature scenes from the game - one is of a battle scene in the game, the other shows the city of Altissia.

I am not an avid gamer, but I have played a few handfuls of games throughout my lifetime. FFXV is definitely one of the best games I have played - mainly because of the breath-taking scenery and the battle system in the game. There are also a number of side quests that the player can optionally complete. These side quests do not progress the story, but they usually help to level up the character, or obtain items that you wouldn't be able to obtain as part of the main story. Usually I don't find side quests entertaining, but the ones in FFXV are quite fun and diverse.

If you haven't picked up a copy of the game I highly recommend that you do! The game is available for purchase at the following links:

FFXV Plot & Trailer

Enroute to wed his fiancée Luna on a road trip with his best friends, Prince Noctis is advised by news reports that his homeland has been invaded and taken over under the false pretense of a peace treaty – and that he, his loved one and his father King Regis, have been slain at the hands of the enemy.

To gather the strength needed to uncover the truth and reclaim his homeland, Noctis and his loyal companions must overcome a series of challenges in a spectacular open world - that is filled with larger-than-life creatures, amazing wonders, diverse cultures and treacherous foes.
