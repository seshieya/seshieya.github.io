Careers > Apprentice Cabinetmaker

Position: Apprentice Cabinetmaker
Terms of Employment: Full time
Anticipated Start Date: As Soon As Possible

We are looking for a full time Apprentice Cabinetmaker, for shop fabrication of cabinetry and doors. If you are willing to learn and have a great attitude, this training position may be for you!

Experience:

  • Knowledge of woodworking
  • At least 1st year Apprentice Cabinet Makers

Type of Work:

  • Construction of Cabinets, Desks, Seating, Panels, Trims, Moldings, Doors
  • Enclosed shop environment

Specific Tasks:

  • Prepare and fabricate wood and wood products into architectural millwork
  • Use of saws, sanders, fastening devices, adhesives, wood working equipment
  • Interpret engineering and design drawings
  • Examine drawings for conformity and errors and provide solutions for quality control
  • Laminate cabinet and furniture components, make jig templates, and process machined parts

Additional Skills:

  • Interaction with cabinetmakers and management to solve problems
  • Occasional lacquer finishing of products
  • Use of Hardware, some experience with solid surface, laminates, and stone
  • Co-ordination of shipping of products with shipping department

Work conditions:

  • Fast paced environment, deadlines, office, and some minor shop
  • Steel toes footwear, tools, safety goggles and hearing protection

How to Apply:
Send your resume and cover letter to office@teamartmillwork.com
