About TeamArt Millwork

TeamArt Millwork specializes in a wide range of custom cabinetry. We have both commercial and residential presence in Greater Vancouver area.

Our client base includes some of the largest contractors in the Greater Vancouver Metro area, such as Fusion Projects, CDC Construction Ltd, and ITC Construction.

TeamArt Millwork is a member of AWMAC. We are fully licensed and insured. We look forward to working with you. Please contact us if you have any inquiries.
